📤Add your own cheats via FTP
  1. Connect an FTP client to your PS5: 
    Host: your-ps5-ip
Port: 2121   ·   User: anonymous   ·   Password: (blank)
  2. Go to /data/elf-arsenal/cheats/ (the folder is created automatically on boot).
  3. Upload your cheat file. Name it <TITLE_ID>.json — e.g. PPSA01411.json. The file's title_id field must also match.
  4. Tap ↻ Refresh in the section above and the new cheats appear when the title is foreground.
